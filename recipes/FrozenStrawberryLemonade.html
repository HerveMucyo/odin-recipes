Frozen Strawberry Lemonade

Description

Strawberries, lemonade and ice are blended into a refreshing slush. A simple
(non-alcoholic) fruity treat, fantastic in any season!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 5 large fresh strawberries, hulled

Steps

  1. Place the ice cubes, water and lemonade concentrate into the container of a
    blender. Process until ice is finely blended. Add strawberries, and puree
    until well blended. Taste, and adjust flavors if you like. This recipe is very flexible.

  2. In a large bowl combine tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, cooled pasta and Italian
    dressing. Refrigerate overnight, or for at least 1 hour.
