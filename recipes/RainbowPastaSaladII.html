Rainbow Pasta Salad II

Description

This is a really quick and easy pasta salad. Great for picnics and BBQ's.
For this quick one, low effort does not make the dish any less impressive.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package tri-colored pasta assortment
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10
    minutes or until al dente; drain and rinse in cold water.

  2. In a large bowl combine tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, cooled pasta and Italian
    dressing. Refrigerate overnight, or for at least 1 hour.
